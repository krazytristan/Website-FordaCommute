EASY AND PERFECT SOLUTION FOR YOUR TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

A public transportation system is a variety of vehicle options - available in Batangas City where the passengers can ride. As for the first timer commuters in Batangas City, it is hard for them to handle and manage to commute alone when people are not familiar with the place but it is not the case because even some people who live in the city do not know all the routes where and where the pick-up/drop-off points in particular vehicles.


FordaCOMMUTE is an website where users can see the routes and terminals of different kinds of transportation modes such as tricycles, jeeps, vans, and buses. It will also display the schedule when drivers begin driving and their last trips of the day. In addition to this, the system also tells the user where the usual pick-up/drop-off areas of different vehicles.
